FitRec Parking

Parking at FitRec is very limited. Members are encouraged to use public transportation whenever possible.
Garage parking is available adjacent to FitRec in the Agganis Arena Parking Garage (Lot A). Alternate parking
is also available at 808 Commonwealth Avenue and in the Langsam garage.

Please Note: Between 6 p.m. and 11 p.m. on non-event days, Agganis Arena parking patrons are asked to pay
the maximum rate UPON ENTRY. Upon leaving, you will be refunded for any unused parking hours.
More specifically, when parking patrons enter the Agganis Arena lot between:

  • 6 p.m. and 7 p.m.: they are asked to pay $10.00
  • 7 p.m. and 8 p.m.: $8.00
  • 8 p.m. and 9 p.m.: $6.00
  • 9 p.m. and 10 p.m.: $4.00
  • After 10 p.m.: $2.00

Again, patrons will be refunded any unused parking fees.

Permit Parking

On non-event dates, Lot A will be available to BU employees with valid Green and Red permits or with Medical
Campus daytime permits (Gold Stickers), on a space-available basis. Evening permits for the Charles River
Campus and the Medical Campus will both be honored after 4 pm.

Non-Permit Parking

On non-event dates, the Agganis Arena parking garage (Lot A) will be available to Fitness & Recreation Center
members and visitors for $2/hr or a maximum of $12/day.
